About

The first in 2018 for US

For one night only, two of the world’s best opera voices, Anna Netrebko (soprano) and Yusif Eyvazov (tenor), will perform in concert. Anna Netrebko has redefined the meaning of being an opera star.

Today’s reigning prima donna, her distinctively beautiful voice, abundant charisma and arresting stage presence make an indelible impression in every role she portrays. Together with her husband Yusif Eyvazov, they make a breathtaking tandem on stage.

The program will feature music by Italian composers Verdi, Cilea, Boito, Mascagni, Puccini, Catalani and more!

Knight Concert Hall

The hall is renowned for world-class acoustics for classical music concerts, built like a room within a room ensuring the hall is completely isolated from ambient sounds and vibrations.
